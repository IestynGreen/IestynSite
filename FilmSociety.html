I established and run the Film Society at Hope university

Photo from the first meeting

Photo of members after a meeting



In October 2021 I established the Film Society Liverpool Hope University and I currently continue to run it as president. The society meets weekly in a lecture theatre to watch and critique a film. Currently, there are 29 members, an average 13 members attending each week and over 100 followers on instagram.

As president I organise the meetings, book the lecture theatre, host surveys and polls, maintain the society's social media (instagram, facebook, email), welcome new members, lead the discussions and attend Student Union meetings.

Running the society has been great fun, and I have really enjoyed meeting lots of new people. I believe it has given me experience in organisation and leadership, and has given me the opportunity to meet and get to know people that I otherwise wouldn't have met.
